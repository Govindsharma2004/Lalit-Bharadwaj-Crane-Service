About Us

We at Lalit Bharadwaj Crane Service offer dependable crane rental services. With a modern fleet and expert operators, we ensure timely and safe operations.

Lalit Bharadwaj Crane Services, established in 2010, is a trusted name in the field of heavy lifting and material handling. Headquartered in Tirthali, Sector 29, Jewar, Uttar Pradesh (203209), with an operational base on Kamra Road, Faridabad, we serve as a leading wholesaler of cranes and a reliable provider of forklift hiring services.

With over a decade of hands-on experience, we have consistently delivered dependable and efficient lifting solutions to construction companies, industrial plants, infrastructure developers, and logistics providers. Our fleet comprises a wide range of modern, well-maintained cranes and forklifts, all operated by professionally trained personnel to ensure the highest levels of safety, speed, and efficiency on every project.

We proudly operate across all regions of India, catering to both small and large-scale projects with precision and commitment.
